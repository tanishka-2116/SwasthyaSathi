# Literature Review

## Overview

The project focuses on preventive healthcare, digital health literacy, and healthcare accessibility in India.

The literature survey examined research papers related to:

- Digital Health Literacy
- Health Chatbots
- Personal Health Informatics
- Preventive Healthcare
- Healthcare Accessibility
- Government Healthcare Services

## Key Findings

### Digital Health Literacy

Research indicates that many citizens face challenges in understanding and applying healthcare information effectively.

### Healthcare Accessibility

Several studies highlight the gap between available healthcare services and public awareness of those services.

### Preventive Healthcare

Research shows that improving health literacy can positively influence preventive healthcare practices.

### Health Informatics

Existing solutions often focus on data collection and monitoring but provide limited personalized guidance.

## Research Gap

Most existing solutions provide healthcare information but do not effectively combine preventive healthcare awareness, personalized guidance, and healthcare resource accessibility into a single user-friendly platform.

This gap motivates the development of SwasthyaSathi.
